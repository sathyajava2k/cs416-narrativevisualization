Do Police Live In the Cities Where They Serve ?

Source:  https://github.com/fivethirtyeight/data/tree/master/police-locals
Article:   https://fivethirtyeight.com/features/most-police-dont-live-in-the-cities-they-serve/
Police Officers Count
The horizantal bar chart displays the number of police officers among the cities with 75 largest police forces.The data collected between 2006 and 2010.

New york has the maximum police forces in the country with 32300 police officers.

This is followed by Chicago, Los Angeles and so on. San Bernardino, California has least of 755 police officers.
Police Locals vs Non-Locals
The bar chart compares percentages of police officers living in the city vs police officers not living in the city.

Hovering over Locals and Non-Locals bar provides the information on the percentages.

Cities can be changed from the dropdown.
Police Locals By Race
The Pie chart compares percentages of police officers by Race living in the city.

For example, in New york only 44.64% of Whites are living in the city, vs 77.09% of Blacks living in the city.

Cities can be changed from the dropdown.
